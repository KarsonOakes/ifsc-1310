Gordo Was Better Than You

He Was in Space

Yes, you heard me correctly, Gordo was in space I know some may think that's a big deal, but to me it's a HUGE DEAL! It takes more than just a normal monkey to go into space. Gordo was twice the monkey you will EVER be. Don't even think about slandering his name by saying But we've sent humans into space, and they came back.

Do You Know What's in Space?

That's right, I bet you don't. It's not what's in space that should scare you, but there are scary things, it's what ISN'T in space that should. Let's go over it:

Air

You like air? Well too bad! Space has no air. Gordo was trained to not only accept that there was no air, but to be without air for hours on end!

But Gordo wore a special suit with air, and the shuttle had air. Wrong People Anonymous

WRONG! Sure, modern technology may be able to do that now, but in Gordo's time, both men and monkies were stronger. They had to train to hold their breath for the entire trip. How long can you hold your breath? 30 seconds to a minute? Ha, Gordo would be so disappointed right now. Not only are you worse at holding your breath, you're also:

  • Physically weaker
  • Mentally weaker
  • At most, half as good at math

Monsters, Probably

You heard me right, there are probably monsters in space, and Gordo had to be ready. He was taught Kung fu as well as the use of a blaster pistol. The terrifying beasts beyond our atmosphere were accustomed to zero gravity, so Gordo was already at a disadvantage. Legends say that he took out at least 2 Zyndwurms and 3 Knarlofects. I doubt you've even heard of them.

He Was On a Mission

The Mission

Gordo's mission was fairly simple:

  1. Take a deep breath
  2. Blastoff!
  3. Meet the king of Earth's atmosphere for permission to cross
  4. Defend the ship from whatever horrors attacked
  5. Reenter Earth's atmosphere
  6. Land and earn at least 5 medals

It was all going well, but one of the Zyndwurms' stars hit his parachute, causing a malfunction. Gordo did what he could to slow the ship, narrowly avoiding a flock of space geese, but the damage was too much. Gordo sadly passed away, his final breath being air from his home planet. He wasn't afraid. He set in motion amazing events that would change history forever.

He Made the Ultimate Sacrifice

The Funeral

Everyone around the world knew of Gordo's death, and his funeral was broadcast all over the world. For a few brief moments, there was complete unity. A hero had fallen. Tears were shed by all but the most heartless people.
